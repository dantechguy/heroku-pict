hi and welcome to danwb-pict!

how to play

  1. you and your friends enter a four digit code above and press 'go to room'. you could also press 'random' which will generate a new room code for you.
  2. everyone needs to enter a name, once everyone has pressed 'ready' the game will continue
  3. everyone will then need to enter a prompt and press 'submit'. this is anything random, for example 'parachuting dog'
  4. these prompts are then shuffled around between users, and everyone attempts to draw their prompt, then pressing 'submit' once ready
  5. these drawings are then shuffled around between users, and all users must write what they think the drawing is, then pressing 'submit'
  6. this cycle of "drawing - writing a prompt - drawing" continues for as many rounds as there are people
  7. at the end of the game, each user receives a summary of where their drawing ended up, showing the chain reaction of events which lead to the final result
